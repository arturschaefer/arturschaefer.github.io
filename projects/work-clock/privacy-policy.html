Privacy Policy

When you use our app, we may collect the following information:

  • Device information such as device ID, IP address, and browser type.
  • Usage data, including pages visited and actions taken within the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide access to the app and its features.
  • Improve app functionality and performance.

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except when required by law, to protect our legal rights, or to ensure user safety.

Children’s Privacy

Data Retention
